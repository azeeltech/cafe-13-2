Our Story

More Than
Just a Café.

Nestled in the vibrant heart of Boring Road, Patna, Cafe 13 (कैफ़े १३) is a sanctuary where youth culture, exquisite taste, and premium hospitality converge.

From the careful roasting of our premium beans to the warm, aesthetically curated interiors, every detail is engineered to make you feel at home. Whether you're sinking into our signature swing seating, sharing laughs with family, completing work in our cozy corners — you're part of something special.
